EchoForge delivers resilient, automated backup/restore across clouds with dashboards, alerting, and compliance workflows.

## Why EchoForge
- Lower RTO/RPO with proven restore paths
- Continuous monitoring and incident automation
- Compliance‑friendly logging and audit exports
